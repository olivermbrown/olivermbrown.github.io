Welcome

Hi, I'm Ollie and I am a PhD student in the School of Mathematics at the University of Bristol. Until 2022, I studied Mathematics and Physics at St John's College, University of Durham. As part of my PhD project I study the quantum theory of anyons on wire networks, with an interest in their relation to topological quantum computing. More generally I am interested in quantum mechanics and quantum information theory.

I have a YouTube channel on which I upload videos about mathematics and theoretical physics, and discuss life at university. In the past, the videos have covered interesting topics in quantum field theory, quantum computing, continuum mechanics, and dynamical systems. Lecture notes for some of these videos will be posted soon.

This site contains papers which I have written and projects I am working on. The painting in the header is of Durham by Stephen Ward Art, whose work can be purchased at https://www.stephenward-art.co.uk/. I believe the view over Durham city centre to be one of the most beautiful and inspiring anywhere in the world.
